Deactivating Account

You have two methods to deactivate your account. “Save Everything” will save your demographics, submitted jokes and ratings for research purposes. Then, you will be unable to access this account unless you have contacted the admin to reactivate it. “Save Nothing” will discard your demographics, submitted jokes and ratings, and it will revoke your participation from the research.
